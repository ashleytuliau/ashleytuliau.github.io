Introduction

Spiced Digital

This magazine is dedicated to young women in careers that incorporate design and technology. It's target audience is women in their 20's and 30's capitalizing on the market's high demand for creatives as well as the booming tech industry. It's the best of both worlds so there's endless opportunity. Content will include tips for female entrepreneurs in tech, articles on the intersection of design and tech, general style and design trends, career advice and market observations, as well as relevant industry news.
